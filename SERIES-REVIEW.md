## PART 6: ORGANIZATIONAL STRENGTHS

### ✅ What's Really Well Done

**1. The Contoso Ltd Scenario**
- Same 50-person company throughout all units
- Same characters (Alex Lee, Casey Kim, Morgan Chen, etc.)
- Realistic but manageable complexity
- Students can imagine themselves in the roles
- Builds cumulative understanding

**2. Troubleshooting Sections**
- Every module has "If X then Y" troubleshooting
- Addresses actual beginner mistakes ("If login fails, then wait ")
- Prevents students from giving up
- Shows instructor understands common issues

**3. Step-by-Step Clarity**
- "Click: [Button Name]"
- "Expected: You see [specific result]"
- No ambiguity about success/failure
- Easy to follow in practice

**4. Consistent Module Structure**
- Every module follows identical template
- Students know what to expect
- Professional and organized
- Easy to navigate to needed sections

**5. Business Value Explanation**
- Every topic explains "Why it matters"
- Not just "How to use ISC"
- But "Why does this solve business problems"
- Beginners see the relevance
