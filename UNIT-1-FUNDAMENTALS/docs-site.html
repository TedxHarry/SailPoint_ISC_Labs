📖 Glossary

Complete Terminology Reference

Comprehensive glossary of SailPoint ISC and identity management terms.

Common Terms

Identity: A unique representation of a person in a digital system.

Access: Permissions and rights granted to an identity in one or more systems.

Provisioning: Creating and configuring user accounts and access rights.

Deprovisioning: Removing user accounts and access rights when no longer needed.

Governance: Controls and processes for managing access and compliance.

Certification: Formal review and approval of access rights by authorized managers.
