## 🎬 Scenario Moments (You'll Encounter These in Labs)

### New Hire Scenario
**Casey Kim** just joined Contoso Finance as an AP Clerk. Your job: automate her onboarding.
- Create Entra ID account
- Add to Finance_Team group
- Provision to Finance App with AP_Clerk role
- Notify Finance Manager
- Schedule first-day access

### Promotion Scenario
**Alex Torres** gets promoted from Sales Rep to Account Executive. Your job:
- Update role from Sales_Rep → Account_Executive
- New access to Salesforce reports
- Notify manager
- Verify no SoD conflicts

### Offboarding Scenario
**Sam Johnson** is leaving Contoso. Your job:
- Disable all accounts (Entra ID, Salesforce, Finance app, etc.)
- Revoke all access
- Archive data
- Create audit trail

### Compliance Review
**Jordan Kim** (Finance Manager) does quarterly access certification:
- Review all Finance team access
- Certify which access is still needed
- Revoke unnecessary access
- Document compliance

## 💡 Tips for Labs

1. **Refer back to this when you see a name** - understand who they are and why they need certain access
2. **Think about their perspective** - "What would Casey Davis want from an access system?"
3. **Use real names in your configurations** - makes labs feel more authentic
4. **Reference these people in your role designs** - "Finance_AP_Clerk role for Casey Kim and others in Finance AP"
