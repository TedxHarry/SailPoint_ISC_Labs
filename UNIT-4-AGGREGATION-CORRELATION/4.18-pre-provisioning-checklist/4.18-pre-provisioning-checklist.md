## 🎓 CERTIFICATION

**Q:** Before moving to provisioning, what is the most critical verification?

A) Aggregation schedule is active
B) Connector status is green
C) ✅ All accounts are correlated to identities AND all attributes are populated
D) Documentation is complete

**Answer: C.** Provisioning depends on correct, complete identity and account data. If accounts unlinked or attributes null, provisioning will fail.

**Q:** You run final reconciliation before Unit 5. It shows "0 discrepancies". What does this mean?

A) Data is corrupt
B) ✅ ISC data perfectly matches source data - ready for provisioning
C) Reconciliation didn't work
D) All accounts are unlinked

**Answer: B.** 0 discrepancies = ISC synchronized with source. Safe to proceed.
