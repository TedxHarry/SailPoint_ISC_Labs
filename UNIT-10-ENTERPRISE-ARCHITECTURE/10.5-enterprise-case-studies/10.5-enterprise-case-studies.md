# UNIT 10.5: Enterprise Case Studies
## Learning from Real-World ISC Implementations

**Difficulty Level:** Advanced | **Time:** ~2 hours

## Learning Objectives
- Learn from enterprise ISC implementations
- Understand common challenges and solutions
- Apply best practices from case studies

## Case Study 1: GlobalBank (Fortune 500)

**Organization:** 25,000 employees, 40 countries

**Challenge:** Legacy identity governance across 100+ systems

**Solution:**
- Federated 4-region ISC architecture
- 18-month phased migration
- 60 system integrations

**Results:**
- Access provisioning: 30 days -> 2 days
- Compliance violations: 5% -> 0.2%
- Admin overhead: -40%

**Key Lessons:**
- Federated architecture for regulatory compliance
- Role consolidation critical for manageability
- Phased migration reduces risk

## Case Study 2: TechStartup SaaS

**Organization:** 2,000 employees, ISC for 500 customers

**Challenge:** Multi-tenant identity governance at scale

**Solution:**
- Hybrid ISC (internal + multi-tenant)
- Automated onboarding workflows
- Self-service portal

**Results:**
- Customer onboarding: 2 weeks -> 2 days
- Support tickets: -60%
- Revenue impact: 20% improvement

**Key Lessons:**
- Automation critical for scale
- Multi-tenancy enables cost efficiency
- Analytics drive improvement

## Case Study 3: Healthcare Organization

**Organization:** 15,000 staff, HIPAA compliance

**Challenge:** Strict compliance, complex role requirements

**Solution:**
- Single HIPAA-compliant ISC instance
- Enhanced audit controls
- Automated compliance certifications

**Results:**
- Audit readiness: 6 weeks -> 1 week
- Compliance violations: 0 (maintained)
- Audit cost reduction: -30%

## Common Success Factors

1. Executive Sponsorship
2. Clear Governance Model
3. Phased Approach
4. Change Management
5. Metrics and Dashboards
6. Automation
7. Testing
