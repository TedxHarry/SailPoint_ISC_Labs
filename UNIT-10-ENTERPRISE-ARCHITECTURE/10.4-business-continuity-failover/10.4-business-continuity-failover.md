# UNIT 10.4: Business Continuity and Failover
## Ensuring Operations Continue During Incidents

**Difficulty Level:** Advanced | **Time:** ~1.5 hours

## Learning Objectives
- Design Business Continuity (BC) plans
- Implement automated failover
- Test recovery procedures
- Communicate during incidents
- Measure recovery metrics

## Business Continuity Framework

### Components

**Prevention:**
- Redundancy (eliminate single points of failure)
- Monitoring (detect issues early)
- Maintenance (prevent degradation)

**Detection:**
- Automated health checks (every 60 seconds)
- Alert escalation (critical alerts to on-call)
- Dashboard monitoring

**Recovery:**
- Automatic failover (< 1 minute)
- Rollback capability (< 5 minutes)
- Communication templates

### RTO/RPO Targets

```
Component        RTO        RPO
────────────────────────────────
Primary ISC      < 1 min    0 min (HA sync)
Database         < 4 hrs    1 hr (backup)
Connectors       < 1 hr     5 min (queued)
Identity data    < 4 hrs    1 hr (sync)
```

### Test Schedule

```
Monthly: Automated tests (no impact)
Quarterly: Failover drill (test environment)
Annually: Full DR exercise (controlled)
```
